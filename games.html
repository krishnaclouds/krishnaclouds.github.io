how to play
  • Players take turns placing their mark — X goes first.

how to play
  • Use arrow keys (desktop) or swipe (mobile) to slide all tiles.
  • When two tiles with the same number collide, they merge into one.
  • Each merge adds the combined value to your score.
  • Reach the 2048 tile to win — but you can keep going for a higher score!
  • The game ends when no more moves are possible.

  • Use arrow keys or WASD to steer the snake.

level 1

how to play
  • Desktop: use ← → arrow keys to move the paddle. The ball launches on first key press.
  • Mouse: moving the mouse over the canvas also controls the paddle.
  • Mobile: touch and drag anywhere on the canvas to move the paddle.
  • Bounce the ball off the paddle to break all the bricks.
  • Don't let the ball fall past the paddle — you have 3 lives.
  • Clear all bricks to advance to the next level. The ball speeds up each level.

how to play
  • ← → move piece  |  ↑ rotate  |  ↓ soft drop
  • Space hard drop — instantly drops the piece to the bottom.
  • C hold — saves the current piece in reserve so you can use it later. Swap it back in by pressing C again.
  • Mobile: use the d-pad buttons below the board.
  • Clear lines to score — 1 line = 100pts, 2 = 300, 3 = 500, 4 (Tetris!) = 800. All multiplied by level.
  • Speed increases every 10 lines. Game over when pieces reach the top.

sudoku

00:00
how to play
  • Click a cell to select it, then press a number key 1–9 or tap the number pad.
  • Fill the grid so every row, column, and 3×3 box contains the digits 1–9 exactly once.
  • Blue numbers are given clues — they can't be changed.
  • A wrong number turns red immediately.
  • ✏ Notes mode — toggle to pencil in candidate numbers without committing.
  • Use arrow keys to navigate cells. Press N to toggle notes mode.
